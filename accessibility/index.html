Accessibility

Accessibility Statement

Our commitment to digital accessibility for all citizens

WCAG 2.1 Level AA Conformant

Our Commitment

Better Solano is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards.

Accessibility Features

Keyboard Navigation

All functionality available using only a keyboard.

Screen Reader Support

Compatible with JAWS, NVDA, and VoiceOver.

Text Alternatives

All images have descriptive alt text.

Color Contrast

Meets WCAG AA contrast requirements.

Responsive Design

Works on all devices and screen sizes.

No Time Limits

No time limits on reading or interacting.

Known Limitations

  • Some PDF documents may not be fully accessible to screen readers
  • Some third-party embedded content may have accessibility issues

Alternative Access

If you encounter difficulty accessing any information, contact us:

volunteer@bettersolano.org

Technical Specifications

HTML5 CSS3 JavaScript React TypeScript ARIA

Our Promise

Better Solano is committed to ensuring that our digital services are accessible to all citizens, regardless of ability. We view accessibility not as a feature, but as a fundamental right.
