Municipal Assessor

Declaration of Land, Building and Machineries

Tax declaration form for real property assessment in Solano, Nueva Vizcaya

Office

Municipal Assessor

Form Type

Tax Declaration

Processing

3-5 Working Days

Location

Municipal Hall

Property Information Required

Details needed for the tax declaration form

Owner Information

  • Full Name of Owner
  • Address / Telephone No.
  • TIN (Tax Identification Number)
  • Administrator/Beneficial User

Property Location

  • Barangay
  • Municipality
  • Province
  • OCT/TCT/CLOA No.

Assessment Details

  • Property Index Number (PIN)
  • ARP No. (Assessment Roll)
  • Effectivity of Assessment
  • Previous ARP No.

Types of Property Declaration

Different categories of real property for tax assessment

Land Declaration

  • Classification (Agricultural, Commercial, Industrial, Residential)
  • Area in Hectares/Square Meters
  • Market Value per Unit Area
  • Actual Use of Land
  • Sub-class and Assessment Level
  • Assessed Value Computation

Building Declaration

  • Kind of Building (Residential, Commercial, etc.)
  • Structural Type and Materials
  • Building Permit No. and Date Issued
  • Date Constructed/Completed
  • Number of Storeys and Floor Area
  • Core and Additional Items

Machinery Declaration

  • Kind of Machinery
  • Brand and Model
  • Capacity/Horse Power
  • Date Acquired/Installed
  • Condition (New, Good, Fair, Poor)
  • Estimated Economic Life

Assessment Summary

  • Total Market Value
  • Assessment Level Percentage
  • Total Assessed Value
  • Taxable/Exempt Status
  • Effectivity Quarter and Year
  • Approving Authority Signature

Documentary Requirements

Documents needed for property declaration

For New Declaration

  • Certified True Copy of Title (OCT/TCT/CLOA)
  • Tax Clearance Certificate
  • Deed of Sale/Transfer Documents
  • Sketch Plan/Location Map
  • Valid ID of Owner

For Building Declaration

  • Building Permit
  • Occupancy Permit
  • Building Plans/Specifications
  • Certificate of Completion
  • Land Tax Declaration

For Machinery Declaration

  • List of Machineries with Specifications
  • Proof of Acquisition/Invoice
  • Installation Permit (if applicable)
  • Building Tax Declaration
  • Business Permit

Municipal Assessor's Office

Handles property assessment, tax declaration, and real property records.

  • Ground Floor, Municipal Hall, Solano
  • Mon-Fri: 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Important Notes

  • Declaration must be filed within 60 days of acquisition
  • Penalties apply for late declaration
  • Keep copies of all submitted documents
