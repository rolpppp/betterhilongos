Business Permit Services

Select a service to view the detailed process and requirements

Issuance of New Business Permit

Application for new business permit for businesses operating in Solano

Requirements for New Business

  • Duly accomplished Application Form
  • DTI Certificate of Business Name Registration (for sole proprietorship)
  • SEC Registration (for corporation/partnership)
  • CDA Registration (for cooperatives)
  • Barangay Business Clearance
  • Zoning Clearance from MPDO
  • Sanitary Permit from MHO
  • Fire Safety Inspection Certificate from BFP
  • Locational Clearance (if applicable)
  • Contract of Lease / Land Title (proof of business location)
  • Community Tax Certificate (Cedula)
  • Valid ID of owner/authorized representative
Step Activity Documents Duration Personnel
1 Submit application form and complete requirements Application form, all requirements 10 minutes BPLS Staff
2 Verification and assessment of documents — 15-30 minutes BPLS Staff
3 Computation of fees and taxes Assessment sheet 10 minutes BPLS Staff
4 Payment of fees at Treasurer's Office Order of Payment 10 minutes Treasurer's Office
5 Issuance of Business Permit Official Receipt 10 minutes BPLS Staff

Processing Information

  • Total processing time: 55 minutes to 1 hour (if complete)
  • Fees vary based on business type, capitalization, and floor area

Renewal of Business Permit

Annual renewal of existing business permit (January 1-20 without surcharge)

Requirements for Renewal

  • Previous year's Business Permit
  • Barangay Business Clearance (current year)
  • Sanitary Permit from MHO (current year)
  • Fire Safety Inspection Certificate from BFP (current year)
  • Community Tax Certificate (Cedula)
  • Sworn Statement of Gross Sales/Receipts
  • Financial Statements (for corporations)
Step Activity Documents Duration Personnel
1 Submit renewal application and requirements Previous permit, clearances 5 minutes BPLS Staff
2 Verification and assessment Gross sales declaration 10 minutes BPLS Staff
3 Payment of fees at Treasurer's Office Order of Payment 10 minutes Treasurer's Office
4 Issuance of renewed Business Permit Official Receipt 5 minutes BPLS Staff
Renewal Period Surcharge
January 1-20 No Surcharge
January 21 onwards 25% Surcharge + 2% Interest/month

Issuance of Mayor's Clearance/Permit

Mayor's clearance for various purposes including employment, travel, and other transactions

For Employment

  • Barangay Clearance
  • Police Clearance
  • NBI Clearance
  • Community Tax Certificate (Cedula)
  • 2x2 ID Photo
  • Valid ID

For Other Purposes

  • Barangay Clearance
  • Community Tax Certificate (Cedula)
  • Valid ID
  • Supporting documents (as applicable)
Step Activity Duration Personnel
1 Submit requirements and fill out application form 5 minutes BPLS Staff
2 Verification of documents 5 minutes BPLS Staff
3 Payment of fee at Treasurer's Office 5 minutes Treasurer's Office
4 Issuance of Mayor's Clearance 5 minutes BPLS Staff
Type of Clearance Fee
Mayor's Clearance (Employment) ₱50.00
Mayor's Clearance (Other purposes) ₱30.00
