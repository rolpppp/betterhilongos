MSWDO Services

Select a service to view the detailed process and requirements

Issuance of Social Case Study Report

For medical, educational, burial, and other assistance programs

Requirements

  • Barangay Certificate of Indigency
  • Valid ID of client/patient
  • Medical Certificate/Abstract (for medical assistance)
  • Hospital Bill/Statement of Account (for medical assistance)
  • Death Certificate (for burial assistance)
  • Funeral Contract (for burial assistance)
Step Activity Fee Duration Personnel
1 Submit complete requirements to MSWDO — 5 minutes Social Welfare Assistant
2 Interview and assessment of client — 15 minutes Social Worker
3 Preparation of Social Case Study Report — 30 minutes Social Worker
4 Review and approval by MSWDO Head — 10 minutes MSWDO Head
5 Release of Social Case Study Report Free 5 minutes Social Welfare Assistant

Processing Information

  • Total processing time: 1 hour 5 minutes
  • Home visitation may be conducted for verification
  • Please accomplish Client Feedback Form and drop at the designated suggestion box

Issuance of Certificate of Indigency

For indigent residents requiring certification for various purposes

Requirements

  • Barangay Certificate of Indigency
  • Valid ID
  • 1x1 or 2x2 ID photo (2 copies)
Step Activity Fee Duration Personnel
1 Submit requirements and fill out application form — 5 minutes Social Welfare Assistant
2 Verification and assessment — 10 minutes Social Worker
3 Preparation and signing of certificate — 10 minutes MSWDO Head
4 Release of Certificate of Indigency Free 3 minutes Social Welfare Assistant

Processing Information

  • Total processing time: 28 minutes
  • Certificate is valid for specific purpose only
  • Please accomplish Client Feedback Form and drop at the designated suggestion box

Assistance to Individuals in Crisis Situation (AICS)

Financial and material assistance for individuals and families in crisis

Requirements

  • Barangay Certificate of Indigency
  • Valid ID of client
  • Medical Certificate/Hospital Bill (for medical assistance)
  • Death Certificate and Funeral Contract (for burial assistance)
  • School Registration/Enrollment Form (for educational assistance)
  • Police Report/Incident Report (for victims of calamity/disaster)
Step Activity Fee Duration Personnel
1 Submit complete requirements — 5 minutes Social Welfare Assistant
2 Interview and assessment of client's situation — 20 minutes Social Worker
3 Preparation of Social Case Study Report — 30 minutes Social Worker
4 Evaluation and approval of assistance — 15 minutes MSWDO Head
5 Release of financial/material assistance Free 10 minutes Social Welfare Assistant

Processing Information

  • Total processing time: 1 hour 20 minutes
  • Assistance amount depends on availability of funds and nature of crisis
  • Home visitation may be conducted for verification

Assistance to Persons with Disability (PWD)

Services and assistance for persons with disabilities

Requirements for PWD ID

  • Barangay Certificate
  • Medical Certificate indicating type of disability
  • 1x1 ID photo (2 copies)
  • Valid ID of applicant or guardian
  • Birth Certificate (photocopy)
Step Activity Fee Duration Personnel
1 Submit requirements and fill out PWD application form — 10 minutes PWD Focal Person
2 Assessment and verification of disability — 15 minutes Social Worker
3 Encoding and processing of PWD ID — 20 minutes PWD Focal Person
4 Approval and signing by MSWDO Head — 5 minutes MSWDO Head
5 Release of PWD ID Free 5 minutes PWD Focal Person

Processing Information

  • Total processing time: 55 minutes
  • PWD ID is valid for 3 years
  • PWD ID entitles holder to 20% discount on goods and services

Assistance to Senior Citizens

Services and programs for citizens 60 years old and above

Requirements for Senior Citizen ID

  • Barangay Certificate
  • Birth Certificate or any valid ID showing date of birth
  • 1x1 ID photo (2 copies)
  • Proof of residence in Solano
Step Activity Fee Duration Personnel
1 Submit requirements and fill out Senior Citizen application form — 10 minutes OSCA Focal Person
2 Verification of age and residency — 10 minutes OSCA Focal Person
3 Encoding and processing of Senior Citizen ID — 15 minutes OSCA Focal Person
4 Approval and signing — 5 minutes MSWDO Head
5 Release of Senior Citizen ID Free 5 minutes OSCA Focal Person

Processing Information

  • Total processing time: 45 minutes
  • Senior Citizen ID entitles holder to 20% discount and VAT exemption
  • Social Pension available for indigent senior citizens (quarterly release)

Assistance to Women

Programs and services for women's welfare and protection

Services Available

  • Solo Parent ID and benefits
  • VAWC (Violence Against Women and Children) assistance
  • Temporary shelter and protection
  • Counseling and psychosocial support
  • Livelihood assistance programs
  • Legal assistance referral
Step Activity Fee Duration Personnel
1 Report/seek assistance at MSWDO — 5 minutes Social Welfare Assistant
2 Interview and assessment of situation — 30 minutes Social Worker
3 Provision of immediate intervention/assistance — Varies Social Worker
4 Referral to appropriate agencies (if needed) Free 15 minutes MSWDO Head

Processing Information

  • Initial response time: 50 minutes
  • VAWC cases are given priority and immediate attention
  • All information is treated with strict confidentiality

Assistance to Children

Child welfare and protection services

Services Available

  • Child abuse/neglect intervention
  • Children in Conflict with the Law (CICL) assistance
  • Foster care and adoption services
  • Day care services
  • Supplemental feeding program
  • Educational assistance
Step Activity Fee Duration Personnel
1 Report/referral received at MSWDO — 5 minutes Social Welfare Assistant
2 Initial assessment and investigation — 30 minutes Social Worker
3 Case conference and intervention planning — 1 hour Social Worker
MSWDO Head
4 Implementation of intervention/assistance Free Varies Social Worker

Processing Information

  • Initial response time: 1 hour 35 minutes
  • Child abuse cases are given immediate priority
  • Coordination with PNP, DSWD, and other agencies as needed
